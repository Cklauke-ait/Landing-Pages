Smarter Lubrication Starts Here

SKF connected lubrication solutions — monitor, control, and optimize every lubrication point across your entire facility.

Close to 70% of bearing failures are due to improper lubrication. Lubrication points are often in hard-to-reach places — sometimes forgotten, sometimes running dry between visits — quietly eroding machine reliability.

SKF's connected lubrication portfolio puts your maintenance team back in control with real-time visibility, automated alerts, and remote monitoring so you can stop failures before they start.
 

Connected Lubrication

SKF SYSTEM 24 / TLDD
Connected Single-Point Lubricator

The SKF TLDD is a wireless connected single-point lubricator that replaces manual setup with remote monitoring and control through the SKF Lubrication Cloud. Built on the proven TLSD platform, it delivers dependable automatic lubrication wherever you need it — in any orientation, indoors or out.

■ Scalable & easy to install — adjustable emptying times from 1 to 12 months, two cartridge sizes
■ Encrypted long-range wireless — secure remote monitoring inside and outside the factory
■ Automatic alarms & notifications — know before a lubricator runs empty
■ Reduces field inspection frequency — reallocate maintenance resources where they matter
■ IP67 gateway option — operates in extreme outdoor environments (heat, cold, rain, snow)
