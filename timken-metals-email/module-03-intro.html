Timken has established itself as a global leader in designing, manufacturing, and applying high-performance engineered bearings for the metals industry. In 2023, Timken joined forces with American Roller Bearing — combining over a century of expertise to deliver a comprehensive portfolio that supports the complete life cycle of metals applications.

Timken’s dedicated metals specialists provide on-site, application-specific expertise — helping customers achieve lower total cost of ownership and extended equipment life, from duty cycle analysis to solutions for evolving environmental factors.
