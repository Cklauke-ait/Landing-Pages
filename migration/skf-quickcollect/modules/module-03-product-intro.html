A smart vibration tool, made simple

SKF QuickCollect™ combines an easy-to-use, portable sensor with a free mobile app to monitor machine health and quickly identify machinery issues and improve reliability before operations are impacted. Acting as a smart vibration tool, the sensor transmits wirelessly to the SKF QuickCollect™ App, instantly provide you with live readings.

  • Easy start-up with no prior training or experience needed
  • All at a cost-effective price point — no need to make the case for capital expenditure
  • Easily download the free SKF QuickCollect™ App from Google Play or the App Store
