SKF Lubrication Cloud

The SKF Lubrication Cloud is a web-based platform accessible from any desktop, tablet, or smartphone. It gives your team a single, consolidated view of every connected lubricator across all assets and sites — with intuitive dashboards, drill-down hierarchy views, and instant alarm notifications.

Real-Time Dashboard

Summary status of all lubricators with alarm, warning, and normal-operation indicators at a glance.

  

Multi-Site Management

Link multiple organizations and sites under one account. Manage all locations from a single login.
 

User Access Control

Role-based permissions with e-mail notifications for daily, weekly, or monthly maintenance summaries.

  

Bulk CSV Upload

Register hundreds of lubrication points in one upload. No complex IT setup — uses GSM network.
